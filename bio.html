Biography



Bina Shah is an artist specializing in sculpture. She grew up in both traditional Iranian and Indian settings, with her formal training taking place in the United States. Her work explores her own personal politics of a person with multicultural roots, and she is often influenced by her Eastern and Western cultural experiences.


Many of her sculptures are made by hand from slabs and coils using both primitive and modern firing techniques. Her sculptural pieces are Raku or saggar fired with metals and oxides. Bina's work explores a variety of themes including transience, containment, beauty, nourishment, and sustenance.
